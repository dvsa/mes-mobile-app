Sign the declarations

Your iPad is locked on this screen so that the candidate can only sign the declaration.

The candidate must sign to confirm that their vehicle is insured and that they live in the UK. They can sign using their finger or the stylus pen. If the signature matches their provisional licence, select ‘continue’. Select ‘retry’ if the candidate needs to re-sign.

You will need to enter your password or touch ID to unlock your iPad and continue.
