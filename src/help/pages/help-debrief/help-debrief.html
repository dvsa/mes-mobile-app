Give a debrief

When the test has finished, select ‘end test’. The debrief will display the candidate’s faults and test result, so you can tell the candidate if they pass or fail.

Give the candidate feedback on their faults and any other comments.

Pass

Give the candidate feedback, their test result and any other comments. After you select ‘end debrief’, you need to take the candidate’s provisional licence and enter their pass certificate number.

Sign the health declaration

Your iPad is locked on this screen so that the candidate can only sign the declaration.

The candidate must sign to confirm their health condition has not changed since they applied for their provisional licence. They can sign using their finger or the stylus pen. Select ‘retry’ if the candidate needs to change their signature.

You will need to enter your password or touch ID to unlock your iPad and continue.

Fail

Give the candidate feedback, their test result and any additional comments. The faults are automatically totalled up are displayed by order of severity.
