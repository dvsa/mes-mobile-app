Eyesight test

To record the eyesight test result, you must select ‘pass’ or ‘fail’. You must end the driving test if the candidate fails their eyesight test.

Tell me question

Press ‘select a question’ to view the tell me question options. If you need to check the wording of any tell me question, select the heading of the question.

You need to select the tick next to the question you ask the candidate, then you need to record whether the candidate’s answer was satisfactory, a driving, serious or dangerous fault from the drop-down box.

Enter the registration number

Use the keyboard to enter the candidate’s vehicle registration number.

Give the vehicle’s transmission

Select if the vehicle has a manual or an automatic transmission.

Test observers

You need to record if anyone observes the candidate’s test. This can be an Approved Driving Instructor (ADI), a DVSA manager (Sup) or any other observer.

You can enter the ADI’s instructor registration number and certificate number.

When you have entered all of the information, select ‘confirm and continue’. You must enter all the mandatory information before you continue, any incomplete sections will be highlighted.
