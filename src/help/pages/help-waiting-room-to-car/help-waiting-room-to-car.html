Check you have the right candidate

You must check each candidate’s details before you start the test.

To view your candidate’s details, select their name from your journal. It will show you the candidate’s contact information, application reference number and driving licence number.

Once you have checked that the candidate’s details are correct, go back to your journal screen and select ‘start test’.

Sign the declarations

Your iPad is locked on this screen so that the candidate can only sign the declaration.

The candidate must sign to confirm they live in the UK and that their vehicle is insured. They can sign using their finger or the stylus pen. If the signature matches their provisional licence, select ‘confirm’. Select ‘retry’ if the candidate needs to change their signature.

You will need to enter your password or touch ID to unlock your iPad and continue.

Complete the eyesight test and Tell me question

At the start of the test, you will need to conduct an eyesight test and ‘tell me’ question. You must also record the vehicle’s registration number, its transmission, whether it’s a school car and if it has dual controls.

You can enter the Approved Driving Instructor (ADI) registration and certification number if you have them.

Eyesight test

To record the eyesight test result, you must select ‘pass’ or ‘fail’. You must end the driving test if the candidate fails their eyesight test.

Tell me question

Press ‘select a question’ to open a modal with the list of Tell me questions. You can click on the heading to display the wording of any tell me question. You need to press the tick at the right, this will then close the modal and display the selected question. You need to record the candidate’s answer, by selected the level of faults (Satisfactory / Driving Fault, Serious, Dangerous).

Enter the registration number

Use the keyboard to enter the candidate’s vehicle registration number.

Give the vehicle’s transmission

Select if the vehicle has a manual or an automatic transmission.

Test observers

You need to record if anyone observes the candidate’s test. This can be an Approved Driving Instructor (ADI), a DVSA manager (Sup) or any other observer.

You can enter the ADI’s instructor registration number and certificate number.

When you have entered all of the pre-test information, select ‘confirm’.

If you do not enter any mandatory information it will be highlighted before you continue.
